Privacy & Confidentiality

Your sessions are protected under UK law, including the Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR. Information shared is confidential and will only be disclosed when legally required (e.g., harm, abuse, neglect). Records are securely stored, and breaches outside legal bounds are unlawful. Clients are informed of these limits upfront for transparency.

Everyone’s experience is unique. I tailor support and therapies like CBT, Person-Centred, and Grounding to suit your needs, working at your pace.
