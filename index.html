About Me

My name is Janine Blake, and I am the founder of Back on Track Counselling. I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy(MBACP) and work in line with their ethical framework to provide safe, professional, and confidential counselling. I understand that reaching out for support can feel daunting, especially when you are feeling overwhelmed, stuck, or unsure where to begin. You do not need to have everything worked out before starting—counselling offers a space to explore what is going on for you, at a pace that feels right. I offer a warm, compassionate, and non-judgemental environment where you can talk openly and be met with empathy and understanding. Whether you are experiencing anxiety, low mood, stress, relationship difficulties, or feeling disconnected from yourself, our work will be centred around your individual needs. At Back on Track Counselling, my aim is to help you feel heard, supported, and empowered to reconnect with your strengths and move forward feeling more grounded and back on track.

Privacy & Confidentiality

All counselling sessions are strictly confidential, ensuring your privacy and trust. We comply fully with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, which means your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. Your data will only be used for the purpose of your counselling and will not be shared without your explicit consent.

Information may only be shared if there is a legal obligation, such as to protect life or prevent serious harm. In such cases, we follow legal and ethical guidelines to ensure your safety and the safety of others. You have the right to access your data, request corrections, and understand how your information is used. Your confidentiality is our priority, and we aim to create a safe, respectful space for your personal growth.
