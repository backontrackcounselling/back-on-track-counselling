About Me

My name is Janine Blake, founder of Back on Track Counselling. I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP).

I offer a warm, non-judgemental, confidential space where individuals can explore emotional challenges at their own pace.

Depression & Low Mood

Stress

Compassionate health-related counselling.

Grief & Loss

Safe, supportive space for processing loss.

Neurodiversity

Neuro-affirming, person-centred therapy.

Professional Experience

I have worked with Open Doors Counselling, Carers Link, and Brain Health, supporting individuals facing emotional and health-related challenges.
