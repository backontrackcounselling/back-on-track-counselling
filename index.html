About Me

My name is Janine Blake, and I am the founder of Back on Track Counselling. I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP), working in accordance with its ethical framework.

I offer a warm, non-judgemental, and confidential environment where individuals can explore emotional difficulties, life challenges, and personal experiences at their own pace. My work is grounded in empathy, respect, and collaboration, supporting you to gain insight, develop resilience, and move forward in a way that feels right for you.
