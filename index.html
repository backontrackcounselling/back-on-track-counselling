About Me

My name is Janine Blake, founder of Back on Track Counselling. I am a registered member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP) and work in accordance with its ethical framework.

I offer a warm, non-judgemental, and confidential space where individuals can explore emotional challenges, life changes, and personal experiences at their own pace, supported with empathy, respect, and care.
