About Us

Welcome to Back on Track Counselling. I am a Member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP), providing a safe, non-judgmental space for individuals struggling with issues like grief, ADHD, anxiety, depression, and stress. I am an empathetic person who is passionate about helping individuals navigate life’s challenges through effective counselling techniques.

My approach to therapy is rooted in Person-Centered Therapy, Solution-Focused Therapy, and Transactional Analysis.

Our Services

At Back on Track Counselling, we offer a range of therapy options tailored to your needs. Our fixed pricing and session times are designed to bring you clarity and ease.

Person-Centered Therapy

This therapy focuses on the individual’s ability to make changes and find their own answers through a non-directive, empathetic approach.

Solution-Focused Therapy

Here, we work together to identify solutions and pathways forward by focusing on your strengths, not just the problems.

Transactional Analysis

This approach helps individuals understand the dynamics of their relationships and their internal thoughts through the analysis of life scripts and ego states.

How Counselling Works

Getting started with therapy is simple and straightforward:

  1. Step 1: Contact me via email or text to book a session or for any initial inquiries.
  2. Step 2: We’ll arrange a time for a consultation or an initial session that works for you.
  3. Step 3: During your first session, we will discuss your goals and concerns, and I will introduce the therapeutic approach that suits your needs.
  4. Step 4: Regular sessions will follow, allowing us to track your progress and address any new issues that arise.
  5. Step 5: At any point, we can review the therapy’s effectiveness and adjust the approach as needed.

If you are unable to reach me directly, please leave your details in the form below, and I will get back to you as soon as possible.

Privacy & Confidentiality

Your privacy and confidentiality are of utmost importance to me. As a professional member of the British Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (MBACP), I adhere to the highest ethical standards. This means:

  • All personal information shared with me will be kept confidential and not disclosed to anyone without your consent, except in situations where disclosure is required by law (such as risk of harm to yourself or others).
  • I will maintain confidentiality about the details of your sessions and the nature of our work together.
  • Your personal data (email, phone number, etc.) will only be used to contact you for appointment scheduling and follow-ups.
  • I am committed to safeguarding your data, following all relevant data protection laws.
