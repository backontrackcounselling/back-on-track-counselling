Support & Therapeutic Approaches

Everyone experiences emotional difficulties differently. Below is an overview of the areas I support and the therapeutic approaches that may help. Counselling is always tailored to you, your experiences, and your pace.

Anxiety

Persistent worry, fear, or physical tension.

Support offered: Person-Centred, CBT-informed, grounding techniques.

Depression & Low Mood

Feelings of sadness, fatigue, or disconnection.

Support offered: Humanistic and CBT-informed approaches.

Stress

Emotional overwhelm or burnout.

Support offered: Solution-Focused Therapy and regulation strategies.

Low Self-Esteem

Self-criticism or low confidence.

Support offered: Person-Centred Therapy.

Grief & Loss

Bereavement or major life changes.

Support offered: Compassionate, non-judgemental counselling.

Neurodiversity

Emotional or relational challenges.

Support offered: Neuro-affirming counselling.
