Privacy & Confidentiality

Your sessions are protected under UK law, including the Data Protection Act 2018 and UK GDPR. Information shared is confidential and will not be disclosed without your consent, unless legally required.

Records are kept securely, following professional standards. Breaches outside legal exceptions are unlawful. Clients are informed of these limits upfront, especially minors and vulnerable adults.

My goal is to provide a safe, confidential environment while respecting your privacy and legal duties to protect life and wellbeing.
